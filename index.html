About Chozhan Foundation

Dedicated to nourishing bodies, educating minds, and supporting special needs communities with compassion and dignity.

Our Story

Chozhan Foundation was born from a simple observation: every child deserves proper nutrition, quality education, and acceptance regardless of their abilities. Founded by parents of special needs children, we understand the unique challenges faced by families.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no child goes hungry, every child receives quality primary education, and children with autism and Down syndrome are embraced, supported, and given opportunities to thrive in their communities.

Our Approach

We believe in holistic support - feeding the body through nutrition programs, feeding the mind through education, and nurturing the spirit through inclusive communities that celebrate neurodiversity and support special needs families.
